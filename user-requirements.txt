TaskN'Budget

Core Features

Task and Project Management:
    Create, assign, and track tasks and subtasks.
    Set deadlines and priorities for tasks.
    Organize tasks into projects or categories.
    Implement a calendar view for task deadlines and reminders.

Budget Tracking and Management:
    Track income and expenses with categories.
    Set monthly or weekly budget limits.
    Visualize spending with charts and graphs.
    Provide insights into spending habits.
